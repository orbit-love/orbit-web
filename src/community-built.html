Hosted by Orbit CEO Patrick Woods and Community Lead Rosie Sherry, Community Built features discussions focused on helping community builders take actionable steps towards building better communities.

You’ll hear real stories of how people are crafting communities from folks who are forging community management best practices.

Tune-in to Episode One with Charlie Ward

Our inaugural guest is Charlie Ward, founder of Weekend Club. What started as a casual meet-up in a London pub has now evolved into an online co-working community that helps keep Indie Hackers accountable. We'll dive into:
  • the idea behind the community
  • how Charlie got his first members
  • how he went from $0 to $2k MRR
  • the challenges of running a paid community alongside a day job
Join us live on Discord on April 15 at 9 am PST. We'll be taking questions on the day.
