Patrick, our CEO, hosts Developer Love, the podcast for people who build developer communities.

You’ll hear from people who win the hearts and minds of developers, including founders, execs and the top minds in developer relations, dev marketing and community management.

Our guests include Brian Douglas, a developer advocate at GitHub and the host of JAMstack Radio, Peggy Rayzis, who leads Developer Experience at Apollo, Tim O’Reilly, Founder, CEO and Chairman of O’Reilly Media, and many more.

The podcast is available on Heavybit Podcasts, Apple Podcasts, Google Play, Soundcloud, Overcast and via RSS.
